Step-by-Step Checkout Process

1 Add Items to Cart

First, you need items in your cart:

  • Browse shops at /customer/shops
  • Click on a shop to view products
  • Click "Add to Cart" on products you want
  • Click the cart icon in the header to view your cart
2 Go to Checkout Page

From the Shopping Cart page:

  • Review your items in the cart
  • Click the Proceed to Checkout button
  • You'll be redirected to the checkout form
3 Fill Customer Information

In the Customer Information section, enter:

  • First Name: Your first name (min 2 characters)
  • Last Name: Your last name (min 2 characters)
  • Phone Number: 10-digit mobile number starting with 6-9
  • Email: Valid email address
4 Enter Delivery Address

In the Delivery Address section, provide:

  • Street Address: Full address with house/flat number (min 10 characters)
  • Landmark: Nearby landmark (optional)
  • Pincode: 6-digit postal code
  • City: City name (default: Chennai)
  • State: State name (default: Tamil Nadu)
üìç Note about Saving Address: The delivery address is automatically saved with your order. In future orders, previously used addresses will be available for selection.
5 Add Special Instructions (Optional)

In the Special Instructions section:

  • Add any delivery instructions
  • Gate codes, floor numbers, etc.
  • This field is optional
6 Review Order Summary

On the right side, you'll see:

  • Shop Name: Where you're ordering from
  • Order Items: List of products with quantities
  • Price Breakdown: Subtotal, delivery fee, discount
  • Total Amount: Final amount to pay
  • Payment Method: Cash on Delivery (COD)
7 Accept Terms & Place Order

Final steps to place your order:

  • Check the I agree to the terms and conditions checkbox
  • The Place Order button will become enabled
  • Click the green Place Order button

8 Order Confirmation

After placing the order:

  • You'll see an order success screen
  • Your order number will be displayed
  • You can track your order status
  • Option to continue shopping or track the order
